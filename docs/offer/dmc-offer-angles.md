## Hormozi's Value Equation — Applied to DMCs

```
Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
```

| Component | What it means for a DMC owner | How we maximize/minimize |
|-----------|------------------------------|------------------------|
| **Dream Outcome** | "I want a steady stream of tour operator partners sending me groups every season" | We position our service as a partnership pipeline, not just "leads" |
| **Perceived Likelihood** | "Will this actually work for MY destination?" | Balkan Wanders proof: 7 calls, 4 sample itineraries, 2 serious. Show the data. |
| **Time Delay** | "How long before I see results?" | "First qualified conversations within 10 days" — proven in pilot |
| **Effort & Sacrifice** | "I'm already overwhelmed running operations" | Done-for-you. 30-min onboarding call, then we handle everything. Zero time from you. |

## 3 Offer Angles for A/B Testing

### Offer A: "Trade Show Killer" (Anchor Against Known Cost)

**Target:** DMCs who attend trade shows (IMEX, IBTM, ITB, WTM)
**Hormozi lever:** Decrease Time Delay + Decrease Effort

**The pitch:**
> You just spent €20K at ITB Berlin. Flights, hotels, booth, team time — for 3 days of speed-dating with whoever walked by. How many of those business cards became real partnerships?
>
> We put your destination directly in front of qualified tour operators — the ones who actually need a ground partner in [destination]. No flights. No booth. No €20K.
>
> We booked 7 qualified calls for a Balkans DMC in 10 days. 4 moved to sample itinerary stage. From a desk in [city], not a trade show floor.

**Pricing:** $2,500/month flat (anchored against trade show cost)
**Guarantee:** "If we don't book at least 5 qualified calls in your first month, month 2 is free."
**Scarcity:** "We work with one DMC per destination to avoid conflicts."

**Why this works (Hormozi):**
- Dream outcome = same result as trade show (partner meetings)
- Time delay = 10 days vs. waiting for next trade show (months)
- Effort = zero (vs. weeks of prep, flights, booth setup)
- Anchoring against €20K makes $2,500 feel like a steal

---

### Offer B: "Risk-Free Pilot" (Pay-Per-Result, Zero Commitment)

**Target:** Skeptical DMCs, first-time lead gen buyers, budget-conscious
**Hormozi lever:** Increase Perceived Likelihood (zero risk)

**The pitch:**
> Most DMCs we talk to have been burned by marketing promises. So we don't ask you to trust us — we ask you to test us.
>
> We'll run a 30-day pilot campaign targeting tour operators in [source market] for your destination. You only pay for qualified calls that actually show up on your calendar.
>
> No retainer. No setup fee. No contract. If it doesn't work, you owe us nothing.

**Pricing:** $100–$150 per booked call. No retainer. No minimum.
**Guarantee:** Implied — you literally only pay for results.
**Scarcity:** "We're running pilot campaigns for 3 DMCs this quarter to build case studies for new destinations."

**Why this works (Hormozi):**
- Eliminates financial risk entirely (effort & sacrifice → near zero)
- "Pay for results" is the strongest B2B guarantee structure
- Perfect for category education — they've never bought lead gen, so remove all friction
- "3 DMCs this quarter" creates soft scarcity without being pushy

---

### Offer C: "Your Sales Arm in [Source Market]" (Partnership Positioning)

**Target:** Established DMCs (25–50 employees) who understand they need international presence
**Hormozi lever:** Increase Dream Outcome (bigger than just leads)

**The pitch:**
> You have the destination expertise. You have the guides, the hotels, the experiences. What you don't have is a sales team in London, New York, or Sydney reaching out to tour operators on your behalf.
>
> We become your digital sales arm in [source market]. Every month, we introduce you to 10–15 qualified tour operators and travel advisors who are actively building programs in your region.
>
> Think of us as your rep at every trade show, every day, without the flights.

**Pricing:** $2,500/month (positioned as a fractional sales hire — cheaper than one employee in London)
**Guarantee:** "Minimum 10 qualified introductions per month, or we extend at no charge."
**Scarcity:** "One DMC per destination per source market."
**Bonus:** "First month includes a free ICP analysis — we'll map exactly which tour operators are the best fit for your destination and specialization."

**Why this works (Hormozi):**
- Dream outcome is BIGGER — not just calls, but an international sales presence
- Anchored against cost of hiring a sales rep ($50K+/year)
- Research confirmed: DMC representation companies offer ~20 qualified introductions/month. We're positioning in the same category but at lower cost.
- "One DMC per destination" is real scarcity — you genuinely can't serve two competing DMCs in Croatia

## Email Structure (Based on Research)

Research says effective cold emails follow this 5-part structure:

1. **Subject line** — 6–8 words, curiosity-driven, personalized
2. **Personalized opening** — reference their destination, a recent event, or something specific
3. **Benefit-centric value prop** — tangible results, not features
4. **Unique differentiator** — what makes us different from generic marketing
5. **Low-friction CTA** — NOT "book a call." Instead: "Worth a quick look?" or "Want me to send the case study?"

Key insight from research: **low-friction offers (downloadable case study) significantly outperform high-commitment CTAs (book a call) in cold email.** So our first email should offer to send the Balkan Wanders case study, not ask for a meeting.

## Guarantee Stack Options

From best to most aggressive:

| Level | Guarantee | Risk for Us | Best For |
|-------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Soft** | "If we don't book 5 calls in month 1, month 2 is free" | Low — extends timeline, doesn't lose money | Retainer clients |
| **Medium** | "Pay per call only. No retainer, no minimum." | Medium — we invest upfront in infrastructure | Pilot clients |
| **Aggressive** | "5 qualified calls in 30 days or you pay nothing" | High — full risk on us | Case study acquisition |
| **God Mode** | "5 calls in 30 days, or you pay nothing AND we give you the lead list we built for free" | Very high | First 1–2 clients only |

For the test campaign: use **Medium** (pay per call) for Offer B, **Soft** for Offer A.
