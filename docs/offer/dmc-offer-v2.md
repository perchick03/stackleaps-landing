## Who This Is For

DMC owners/managers who want qualified tour operator introductions but don't have time, budget, or systems for outbound sales.

**Primary target (highest conversion from pilot data):**
> Small/boutique tour operator-facing DMC (5-25 employees) with a specific activity or destination niche, operating across multiple destinations, positioning around authentic/immersive/off-beaten-path experiences.

**Secondary target:**
> MICE/events DMC wanting corporate and incentive planner introductions in specific source markets.

**Deprioritize:**
- Large DMCs (50+ employees) — they have in-house sales teams
- Pure luxury without a niche — "luxury" alone is not a conversion signal
- Single-destination specialists showing no expansion signals

## The Core Problem (Universal — Not Trade-Show Specific)

DMCs are invisible to the operators who'd love to work with them.

This isn't a trade show problem. It's a structural one:

1. **No outbound capacity** — You're managing ground logistics, client experiences, and supplier relationships. You're also supposed to be doing sales and marketing on top of it.
2. **Referrals don't scale** — Your best partnerships came from chance meetings or word-of-mouth. Great — but you can't control when the next one comes.
3. **Digital noise** — Your Instagram gets lost. Your website ranks behind 233 million other results. Tour operators who'd be a perfect fit don't know you exist.
4. **Disintermediation** — OTAs and direct booking platforms erode your traditional role. You need direct TO relationships they can't touch.

These pain points apply equally to a 5-person adventure DMC in Montenegro, a luxury cultural DMC in Italy, and a MICE operator in Dubai.

## The Offer: Partner Pipeline Pilot

### What the DMC Gets

1. **Custom ICP Analysis Report** — We map the 50 best-fit tour operators, travel advisors, and event planners for your destination and niche. This is yours to keep regardless of what happens next.

2. **Done-for-you personalized outreach** — Every message is tailored to the operator and your destination. No templates. No mass blasts. The kind of outreach that gets a 14.5% positive reply rate.

3. **3 qualified introductions** booked directly on your calendar as proof of quality. These aren't scraped leads — they're real conversations with decision-makers who are interested in your destination.

4. **Post-meeting follow-up templates** — 3 email templates for nurturing the relationship after the call. Solves the "what do I say next?" problem.

5. **Destination exclusivity** — We never work with a competing DMC in your destination. Once your area is taken, it's taken. Your competitive advantage stays protected.

### What It Costs

| Component | Amount | What It Covers |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| **Setup fee** | $750 (one-time) | ICP research, campaign infrastructure, domain setup, targeting |
| **Per meeting** | $150/booked call | Qualified introductions that show up on your calendar |
| **Contract** | None | No minimum. No lock-in. Walk away anytime. |

### The Risk Reversal

> We deliver your first 3 qualified introductions. If you're not happy with the quality of the conversations — keep the ICP report, shake hands, walk away. You're out $750 for research you can use yourself. If you ARE happy — we keep going at $150 per meeting.

**Why this works:**
- $750 buys something tangible (ICP report + infrastructure) — not "a retainer"
- 3 calls is enough to prove quality without over-committing either side
- $750 risk is trivial compared to a trade show ($15K-$40K) or hiring a rep company
- Charging for the pilot filters for serious buyers (research confirms free trials attract tire-kickers)
- Explicit exit clause removes "trapped in a contract" fear

### What We Need From the DMC

1. 30-minute onboarding call (destination, specialization, ideal partner profile, source markets)
2. Sample itineraries or portfolio to reference in outreach
3. Calendar access for booking introductions
4. Respond to booked calls within 24 hours

**Phase 1 (Now — First 3-5 clients):**

**Phase 2 (After 3-5 case studies):**
- $1,500 setup + $200/call, or transition to $2,500/mo retainer + $100/call hybrid

**Phase 3 (Scale):**
